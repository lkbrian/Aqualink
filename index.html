Services

Aquilalink Solutions LTD provides diverse, cutting-edge tech solutions for businesses.

Web Application

We develop modern web apps using the latest technologies like react.js, vue.js, angular and other solutions to provide blazing fast and a modern experience.

Agriculture Management System

Our AMS software caters specifically to the agricultural industry, encompassing the entire cultivation process from land recruitment and farmer management to harvesting, weighing, and transportation, addressing challenges such as fraud and scheduling. It offers complete automation and integrates seamlessly with our ERP system.

Mobile Apps

We deliver modern app using cutting edge technologies like react native, expo, ionic, flutter. We strongly believe in the importance of delivering multi-platform mobile app solutions for android/ios/windows and still able to provide quick turn around and retain native app experience.

eCommerce

Looking to quickly run your business online on a budget or to set up a full-service eCommerce store for your customers. We work with industry-leading solutions like Magento, Shopify, WooCommerce to get your store up and running.
